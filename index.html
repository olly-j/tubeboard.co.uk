Data and trust

Independent, transparent, and built for quick checks.

TubeBoard is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport for London. Live arrival information is powered by TfL Open Data where available, with clear source attribution and privacy details available before the App Store launch.

Live data source

TfL Open Data powers the planned live arrivals experience where data is available. TubeBoard keeps the product independent and avoids official TfL branding or endorsement claims.

Roadmap-only until implemented

iOS ecosystem features should stay honest.

Widgets, Live Activities, Dynamic Island, Apple Watch, and disruption alerts should appear in App Store assets only after they are present in the launch build.

FAQ

Is TubeBoard official?

No. TubeBoard is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Transport for London.

Does it use live data?

TubeBoard is designed to use TfL Open Data for live arrivals where available.

Can I save my usual stations?

Favourite stations are part of TubeBoard's core product direction, designed to keep regular stops close for quick checks.

Where can I get support?

Use the support page for launch status and help information. Privacy details are available in the privacy policy.
