Launch Status

TubeBoard is preparing for App Store / TestFlight release. Until the App Store listing is live, the app is coming soon.

Contact

Email support@tubeboard.co.uk with your device model, iOS version, station name, and a short description of the issue.

Data Availability

TubeBoard is designed to show London Tube arrivals using TfL Open Data where available. If live data is delayed or unavailable, the app experience may be limited until the source data recovers.

Independence

TubeBoard is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport for London.
