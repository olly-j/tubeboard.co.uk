Privacy

Privacy policy.

TubeBoard keeps the app simple: no account, no website cookies, and favourite stations stored on your device.

App Data

No account is required to use TubeBoard. Favourite stations are stored on your device.

Website

This website does not use cookies, account sign-in, advertising trackers, or newsletter forms.

Data Source

The app requests TfL data for selected stations so it can show live arrivals powered by TfL Open Data.

Live Activities

Live Activity push tokens may be sent to the TubeBoard backend when Live Activities are enabled, so the app can support updates through Apple's push notification service.

Updates

This policy will be updated if the app adds analytics, crash reporting, location features, or any other personal-data behavior.
