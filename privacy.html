Privacy

Privacy policy.

TubeBoard is preparing for launch. This website does not use cookies, account sign-in, advertising trackers, or newsletter forms.

App Data

TubeBoard is designed for quick London Tube arrival checks. The launch app should only collect data needed to provide the product, such as station selections, favourite stations, diagnostics, or live arrival requests. Final App Store privacy details will match the released app build.

Data Source

Live arrival information is planned to use TfL Open Data where available. TubeBoard is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport for London.

Updates

This policy will be updated before the App Store release if the app adds analytics, crash reporting, location features, or any other personal-data behavior.
